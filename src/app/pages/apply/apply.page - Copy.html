First - Are you a Part of the Pacifica Network?

To apply to be on Pacificaster, you must be a part of an Affiliate, or Independent Producer of the Pacifica Network.

You can apply to be a part of the Pacifica Network using the form here:
https://pacificanetwork.org/joinpacifica/

 

Apply for Pacificaster

If you are a part of the Pacifica Network, you can apply for your podcast with the forms below. With our application, you will enter in your information and provide a sample episode. We will listen to what you've sent us, for us to give you our approval. We will send an email with a link to your account, where you can set yourself up. We have separate applications for Affiliates and Independent Producers, so make sure you click on the right one.
